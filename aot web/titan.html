What is the release date of
Attack on Titan Season 4?

It has now been revealed that the fourth and final season
Attack on Titan will premiere on December 7th in Japan
“later this year” on Crunchyroll. In the build up to season 4,
an Attack on Titan original movie that will recap seasons
1-3 called 'Chronicle' will premiere on November 18th.
